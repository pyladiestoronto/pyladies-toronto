January 24th, 2019¶

Our PyLadies 2019 kick-off was held at Instacart’s Toronto office and was a great success!

The line-up:¶

  • Migrating from Python 2 to Python 3: Lessons Learnt and Pitfalls Navigated. Presented by Kristen and Mariam.
  • Empowering Patients through Machine Learning Driven Personalized Digital Health Interventions. Presented by Mareena. See slides here.
  • A Background Erasor Tool in Python. Presented by Deepika. See slides here.
  • Know Your Editor and Tools. Presented by Anna.
